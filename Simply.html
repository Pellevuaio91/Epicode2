Focaccia with Caramelized onions,
Cranberries and Bries

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onion, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favrite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes(scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired,though it's not
    necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

In a medium size mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment,mix together all
the dough ingredients until the flours is fully absorbed.You dont'need to actually knead the
dough, though i sometimes knead it a few minutes in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate
the last of the flour...
